原产地介绍
Descriptions of Origin

福建位于中国大陆东南沿海，与宝岛台湾隔海相望。福建属于亚热带季风气候，夏季常有暴雨台风光顾，让人冷得发抖的冬季只有短短十几天。很少下雪。兰麒锋生于龙岩，长于厦门，兼蕴山峦和海洋的品性。
Fujian Province is located on the southeast of mainland China, devided from Taiwan by Taiwan Strait. Fujian is accustomed to the frequent greetings of typhoons and thunderstorms in summer, while the cold-to-death winter is short and it almost never snows. Lan was born in Longyan, raised in Amoy, which grants him the temperaments of mountains and ocean.

Feature One: Volleyball

他第一次接触排球已经是高三。起初，他只是喜欢当时的排球老师。可后来随着时日流转，那颗球，那张网，那块9×18的场地，已经占据了他内心的大部。再后来，排球带来的团队合作的魅力，向上飞翔的追求，得分的狂喜，逐渐令他痴迷于此。
His first acquaintance with volleyball was in his senior. At first, he played it only for his volleyball coach. As time flew, however, the ball, the net, the 9×18 court, has occupied a great part in his heart. And then, the charm of teamspirits, the pursuit to flying high, the ecstasy of scoring, all that volleyball brang to his, have his magnetized so deeply.

附属特性三：电音男孩
Feature Three: Electronic Music Boy

他初中开始喜欢上电子音乐。他的电音世界的领路人是组合The Chainsmokers。他的口味从最初较为清淡、潮流的EDM，Tropical House，到Progressive House，Future Bass，再到比较硬核的Techno，Dubstep，再到现在小众的Glitch Hop和实验电子，偶尔听一些优质的Trap和Bounce。
他曾经担任高中电子音乐社的社长，学习过一段时间的FL Studio和打碟，差点就在学校运动会上上台表演。现在由于电子音乐市场的商业化、同质化现象过于严重，他只有在遇到极其优质的作品时才会想起这项曾经为之狂热的爱好
The story dated back to his junior high. The leader to his EM world is band--The Chainsmokers. These years witnessed a transformation of his flavor. First it was EDM and Tropical House that were hot and mild at that time. Then it was Progressive House, Future Bass. Next hardcore Techno and Dubstep. Now, his flavor is anchored at Glitch Hop and Experimental, which have a smaller lover population.Plus some Trap and Bounce of high quality.
He was the founder of school EM club. He has learned FL Studio and Mixing a long time ago, scarcely able to perform in Sports Meeting stage. The over commercialized and homogeneous EM market has been bothering him for a long time. He could barely think of this hobby he used to be mad at when encountering a really good piece.
