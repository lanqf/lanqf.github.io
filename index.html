附属特性三：电音男孩
Feature Three: Electronic Music Boy

他初中开始喜欢上电子音乐。他的电音世界的领路人是组合The Chainsmokers。他的口味从最初较为清淡、潮流的EDM，Tropical House，到Progressive House，Future Bass，再到比较硬核的Techno，Dubstep，再到现在小众的Glitch Hop和实验电子，偶尔听一些优质的Trap和Bounce。

Now, his flavor is anchored at Glitch Hop and Experimental, which have a smaller lover population.Plus some Trap and Bounce of high quality.

其他属性
Other Characters

以下补充他的一些其他零碎的属性。
有些成为了他的核心竞争力，有些却又令其苦恼，有些能够与他和谐相处、为其所用，有些却怎么也摆脱不掉。
无论如何，这些属性在可预见的将来都将附于其身，他要做的就是认识他自己。
Below are some of his other characters.
Among them, some build up his core competence, some bother him a lot, some are in harmony with him and good usage by him, some adhere to him tightly. Anyway, these characters will attach to him for a long time. All he need to do is to know himself.

双子座
Gemini

正如双子座的特性，他常常天马行空，思维跳跃，话说到一半可能就跳到另一个主题去了，总是令旁人感到不解。但同时他也保有双子座人典型的充沛的想象力。
As a Gemini, his mind runs fast. Sometimes dozens of topics will be included in his one speech, leaving others puzzled.In the meanwhile, he consrves abundant imagination that is typical of Gemini.

身高
Height

他常常因自己的身高而焦虑自卑。作为一名排球爱好者，没有令人侧目的身高是硬伤。这也是他选择打二传手的一部分原因。
He has been feeling nervous and inferior for his 180 height. As a volleyball fan, it's so sad not having an outstanding height. BTW, it was part of the reasons why he played setter.

梦想家
Dreamer

他几乎每晚都做梦。但是现实中，有时他会为了目标一意孤行，为了热爱奋不顾身，有时也会面对向往畏畏缩缩，错失良机。矛盾体了属于是。
He almost dreams every night, of which he is so proud.But in real life, there're so many contradictions. Sometimes he went straight ahead and disregard of anything for his love, while sometimes he surrenders to obstacles and miss the shot.

5G 冲浪
5G Surfer

作为互联网的原住民，他每天25小时高强度网上冲浪，紧追时事热点。多平台结合给予他广阔的视野，而对B站的深耕又让他在年轻人的领域话题格外擅长。自然地，他熟习当前互联网上的热梗、热话题。
He has a good command of current hot memes and hot topics. As a netizen, he surfs the Internet 25h a day, following hot topics. The diversity of platforms grants him a broadened horizen, while an aparment in Bilibili makes him particularly good at the regions of young people.

科幻爱好者
Sci-fi Lover

他最喜爱的科幻作家是刘慈欣。他偏爱强的逻辑性与科学性的硬科幻。科幻作品中的科学性正说明小说中的场景理论上是有可能发生的，这常常引发他的思考，同时这种亦真亦幻的感觉也令他着迷。
His favorite sci-fi writer is Liu Cixin. His preference is hardcore sci-fi with strong logic chain and scientific basic. It reveals that the scenes in book have the posibility to happen some day theoretically, which elicits his reflection and charm him a lot.

饮食清淡
Mild Food

辣的食物不仅无法为他带来特有的酣畅淋漓的痛快感，反而会使他承受生理和精神双重痛苦。油的、咸的、重口的食物也会造成不愉悦感，破坏用餐体验。清淡饮食对他来说呈现食物本真风味的同时，又有益健康，何乐不为？
Spicy food not only fails to bring about pleasure, but causes biological and mental agony. Food with too much oil, salt, strong flavors also makes him unpleasant, distroy dining experience. Mild food displays the original flavors of food and is good for health. Why not?
